AutoZone ITLA is a comprehensive vehicle management app built with Flutter, designed for the Dominican automotive community. Whether you're logging a fuel stop, recording maintenance work, monitoring tire health, or browsing the latest automotive news, AutoZone ITLA keeps all your vehicle data organized and accessible from your phone.

## How to get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    Download and install the AutoZone ITLA APK on your Android device. See [Setup](/getting-started/setup) for step-by-step instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Register using your ITLA student ID. You'll receive a temporary token to complete account activation. See [Authentication](/getting-started/authentication).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first vehicle">
    After logging in, navigate to **My Vehicles** and tap the **+** button to register your vehicle with its plate, chassis, make, model, and year.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start tracking">
    Log your first maintenance record, fuel stop, or expense to begin building a complete picture of your vehicle's history and costs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Several features — including the forum browser, vehicle catalog, news feed, and educational videos — are available without signing in. Create an account to unlock full vehicle management capabilities.
</Note>
