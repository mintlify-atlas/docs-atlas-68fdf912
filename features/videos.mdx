The **Videos Educativos** section gives you a library of automotive how-to videos sourced from YouTube. You can browse the full gallery, filter by category at a glance, and watch any video without leaving the app. No login is required.

## Video gallery

Videos are displayed in a two-column grid. Each card shows the video's `thumbnail` as a full-width preview image with a play icon overlaid at the center, and below it the `titulo` and `categoria`. Tapping a card opens the in-app player.

Each video in the gallery is represented by a `VideoItem` with the following fields:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| `id` | Unique identifier for the video entry |
| `youtubeId` | The YouTube video ID used to initialize the player |
| `titulo` | Video title, shown on the card and in the player screen |
| `descripcion` | Extended description shown below the player |
| `categoria` | Topic category shown in the app's accent color |
| `url` | Full YouTube URL for the video |
| `thumbnail` | Preview image URL loaded from YouTube |

## Watching a video

Tap any card to open the **VideoPlayerScreen**. The `youtube_player_flutter` package initializes a `YoutubePlayerController` using the video's `youtubeId` and begins playback automatically. A red progress bar runs along the bottom of the player while the video plays.

Below the player you can see the `titulo`, `categoria`, and full `descripcion` for the selected video.
