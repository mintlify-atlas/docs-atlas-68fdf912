---
title: "Log fuel and oil entries per vehicle in AutoZone ITLA"
description: "Record every combustible fill-up and aceite change with the quantity, unit of measure, and the amount paid in RD$ for accurate cost tracking."
---

The Combustible & Aceite section tracks two types of fluid entries for each vehicle: **combustible** (fuel fill-ups at the pump) and **aceite** (oil top-offs or full oil changes). Both entry types share the same form and list view, organized into two tabs so you can see them separately.

## Entry types and their fields

Every combustible or aceite entry stores the following:

| Field | Type | Required | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| `vehiculo_id` | integer | yes | Set automatically from the vehicle you navigate from |
| `tipo` | string | yes | Either `combustible` or `aceite` |
| `cantidad` | number | yes | Volume of fluid added |
| `unidad` | string | yes | Unit of measure — see options below |
| `monto` | number | yes | Amount paid in RD$ |
| `fecha` | date | no | Recorded at creation time |

**Unidad options by type:**

- For **combustible**: `galones` or `litros`
- For **aceite**: `qt` (quarts) or `litros`

## Logging a new entry

Navigate to a vehicle's detail screen, tap **Registros de Combustible**, then tap the **+** button. The form opens on whichever tab is currently active — Combustible or Aceite.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose the entry type">
    At the top of the form, a segmented button lets you switch between **Combustible** and **Aceite**. Select the one that matches what you are recording. Switching the type also resets the **Unidad** selector to the appropriate default for that type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the quantity">
    Type a number in **Cantidad**. This is the volume you added — for example, `10` for ten gallons of gasoline, or `4` for four quarts of oil.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the unit">
    Choose from the **Unidad** dropdown. For combustible the options are `galones` and `litros`; for aceite the options are `qt` and `litros`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the amount paid">
    Type the total cost in **Monto (RD$)**. Decimal values are accepted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the entry">
    Tap **Registrar**. The entry is saved and you are returned to the list, which refreshes automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing and filtering entries

The list screen uses two tabs — **Combustible** and **Aceite** — to separate the two entry types. Each tab shows its own scrollable list of records.

Each list item displays:
- The **cantidad** and **unidad** as the title (e.g. "10.0 galones")
- The **fecha** as the subtitle
- The **monto** aligned to the right in RD$ (e.g. "RD$ 850.0")

Pull down on either tab to refresh its records. Tap the **+** button while on the Combustible tab to pre-select `combustible` as the type; tap it from the Aceite tab to pre-select `aceite`.

<Note>
  The combustible and aceite totals both feed into the financial summary on the vehicle detail screen. They are combined into a single "Combustible" total in the summary view.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Log entries immediately after each fill-up or oil change to keep your cost-per-period calculations accurate in the vehicle's financial summary.
</Tip>
