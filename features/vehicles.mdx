The Vehicles section is your home base in AutoZone ITLA. Every other feature — maintenance records, fuel logs, tire status, expenses, and income — lives under a specific vehicle, so registering your fleet is always the first step.

## Adding a vehicle

Tap the **+** button on the Mis Vehículos screen to open the registration form. All fields except the photo are required.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter the plate and chassis number">
    Fill in **Placa** (license plate) and **Chasis** (chassis/VIN). These two fields uniquely identify the vehicle in the system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in make, model, and year">
    Enter **Marca** (brand, e.g. Toyota), **Modelo** (e.g. Corolla), and **Año** (four-digit year). The year field only accepts numeric input.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the wheel count">
    Select **Cantidad de Ruedas** from the dropdown. The available options are 2, 4, 6, 8, and 18. The default is 4. This value determines how many tire positions are tracked under this vehicle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the vehicle">
    Tap **Registrar Vehículo**. The app submits the record and returns you to the list, where the new vehicle appears immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a photo (optional)">
    Open the vehicle's detail screen and tap the camera icon in the bottom-right corner of the photo area. The app opens your device gallery. Select an image and it uploads automatically. Images up to 20 MB are accepted; the server compresses them to a maximum of 100 KB.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You can register a vehicle without a photo and add one later from the detail screen.
</Note>

## Browsing your vehicles

The **Mis Vehículos** list shows all vehicles registered to your account. Each card displays the vehicle photo (or a placeholder icon), the make and model as the title, and the year and plate as the subtitle.

Pull down on the list to refresh it, or tap the refresh icon in the top-right corner. Tap any card to open the vehicle's detail screen.

## Vehicle detail and financial summary

The detail screen shows all the fields you registered — Marca, Modelo, Año, Placa, Chasis, and Ruedas — and pulls a financial summary from the server. The summary covers:

| Concept | Description |
|---|---|
| **Mantenimientos** | Total spent on maintenance records |
| **Combustible** | Total spent on fuel and oil entries |
| **Gastos** | Total of categorized expense entries |
| **Ingresos** | Total income earned from the vehicle |
| **Balance** | Ingresos minus all costs combined |

<Tip>
  A positive balance means the vehicle is generating more than it costs. A negative balance is a signal to review your expense categories.
</Tip>

## Editing a vehicle

From any vehicle's detail screen, tap the **edit** icon (pencil) in the top-right corner. The edit form loads with all current values pre-filled. Update any combination of Marca, Modelo, Año, Placa, Chasis, or Cantidad de Ruedas, then tap **Guardar Cambios**.

To update the photo, tap the camera icon on the detail screen at any time — you do not need to open the edit form. The gallery picker opens and the new image replaces the previous one.

<Warning>
  Deleting a vehicle removes it permanently along with all of its associated records. Use the trash icon on the detail screen and confirm the dialog to proceed.
</Warning>
