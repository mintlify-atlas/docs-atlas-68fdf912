The **Noticias Automotrices** section keeps you up to date with what is happening in the Dominican automotive world. The app fetches the latest articles from the server and displays them in a scrollable list. No account is required to read any article.

## News list

The list shows up to 10 articles, ordered by recency. Each card displays:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| `imagenUrl` | Hero image for the article, displayed full-width at the top of the card |
| `titulo` | Article headline |
| `fecha` | Publication date |
| `resumen` | Short summary shown below the date, up to three lines |

Scroll through the list and tap any card to open the full article.

## Reading an article

Opening a card loads the article detail screen. The screen shows:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hero image">
    The article's `imagenUrl` is displayed at 250 px tall and full width above the content area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Title and date">
    The `titulo` appears as a large headline below the image, with `fecha` in smaller text beneath it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Full content">
    When the article has a `contenido` value, it is rendered in a built-in web view using the `webview_flutter` package. This preserves the original HTML formatting, including images, links, and styled text. If no `contenido` is available, the `resumen` is shown as plain text instead.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  News content is curated specifically for the Dominican automotive market. Articles cover local regulations, regional vehicle trends, and news relevant to drivers in the Dominican Republic.
</Note>

<Tip>
  If an article image fails to load, the app shows a placeholder automatically — the rest of the article still loads normally.
</Tip>
