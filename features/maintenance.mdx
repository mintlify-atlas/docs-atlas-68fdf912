The Maintenance section gives you a complete service history for each vehicle. Every time you take a car in for a checkup, oil change, brake job, or any other work, you can record what was done, what it cost, which parts were replaced, and attach photos of the job.

## What a maintenance record contains

Each record stores the following fields:

| Field | Type | Required | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| `vehiculo_id` | integer | yes | Set automatically from the vehicle you navigate from |
| `tipo` | text | yes | Free-form description, e.g. "Cambio de aceite", "Cambio de frenos" |
| `costo` | number | yes | Cost in RD$ |
| `piezas` | text | no | Parts used during the service, e.g. "Filtro de aceite, aceite 5W-30" |
| `fecha` | date | yes | Date the service was performed (YYYY-MM-DD) |
| `fotos` | images | no | Up to 5 photos attached after saving |

## Logging a new maintenance record

Navigate to a vehicle's detail screen and tap **Ver Mantenimientos**, then tap the **+** button.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter the maintenance type">
    Type a description in **Tipo de Mantenimiento**. This is a free-text field — write whatever describes the work, such as "Alineación y balanceo" or "Cambio de correa de distribución".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the cost">
    Type the amount in **Costo (RD$)**. The field only accepts numeric input. Decimal values are supported.
  </Step>
  <Step title="List parts used (optional)">
    In **Piezas utilizadas**, enter the components replaced or consumed during the service. This field accepts multiple lines, so you can list each part on its own line.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the date">
    Tap the **Fecha** field. A date picker opens — select the day the maintenance was performed and confirm. The date is stored in YYYY-MM-DD format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach photos (optional)">
    Tap the **+** tile in the photo section to open the gallery. You can attach up to 5 images. Each added photo appears as a thumbnail; tap the **×** on any thumbnail to remove it before saving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the record">
    Tap **Registrar Mantenimiento**. The app first saves the core record, then uploads any selected photos. You are returned to the maintenance list on success.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Photos are uploaded separately after the record is created. If the upload step fails, the record itself is still saved — you can re-attach photos later if needed.
</Note>

## Viewing the maintenance list

The maintenance list screen shows all records for the selected vehicle in reverse-chronological order by default. Each card displays:

- The **tipo** as the title
- The **fecha** and **costo** (e.g. "2025-03-14 • RD$ 2500") as the subtitle

Pull down to refresh the list. Tap any card to open its detail view.

## Maintenance detail and photo gallery

The detail screen shows the full record:

- **Tipo** as the headline
- **Fecha** below the headline
- **Costo** with a currency indicator in RD$
- **Piezas Utilizadas** (only shown when the field is not empty)

If the record has attached photos, a carousel appears at the top of the screen. Swipe left and right to move between images. The carousel fills the full width of the screen at 250 px height.

<Tip>
  Before a big service, take a photo of the vehicle's odometer and include it in the maintenance record. This gives you a mileage reference for every entry without needing a separate odometer field.
</Tip>
